The e-Team

The e-Team is composed of professionals coming from diverse cultural backgrounds and with a rich variety of skills and expertise.

We are all volunteering our time to the e-Luminate™ Festival whilst juggling the demands of our other businesses, jobs, and family lives. We do so because we believe in this project and we want to make a real difference in the city promoting a more collaborative approach to work.
 

The board

Chairman: Hugh Parnell is a passionate supporter of environmental, low carbon and renewable energy technology companies, Chairman of Cambridge Cleantech & of Cambridge Energy Forum, adviser on angel/VC equity and corporate finance for early stage business growth, ambassador for Wysing Arts.

Managing Director: Alessandra Caggiano is an enthusiast networker, guided by an irresistible entrepreneurial spirit. She makes the most of every encounter and every minute. Passionate about art, technology, and cultures, she wishes to improve the collaboration amongst various sectors to promote innovation!

David Gill is Managing Director of the St John’s Innovation Centre in Cambridge, the longest-established business incubator in Europe for technology ventures. He previously set up and ran the Innovation & Technology Unit at HSBC Bank in London (1997-2004), before serving as an executive director of a venture fund focused on early-stage, technology-based investments. Educated at Cambridge, he qualified as a barrister before working in corporate finance for US and UK banks.
     A Sloan Fellow at the Stanford Graduate School of Business in California (2005), David is currently an Academic Collaborator at the University of Cambridge Institute for Manufacturing, a non-executive director of UK Business Incubation Ltd and a member of the Department for Business Access to Finance Expert Group.

The e-team

Artistic Director: Ross Ashton is a creative director and projection artist. He is a practitioner of the art of Architectural Projection. He is an expert at using images and architecture in combination to allow audiences to see a building in a new way. He has been associated with prestigious events all over the world.

Operations Director: Begonia Forcada is a creative individual who sees culture and arts as key drivers for innovation and collaboration leading to new ventures. She is a novice Cello player, and she brings her vitality and professionalism to the festival.

Marketing Director: Jules Abensour is a digital and traditional marketing specialist who loves creating a buzz around innovative brands and products. She's particularly interested in start-ups, and enjoys finding creative, high-impact solutions to new business challenges.

Events Coordinator: Alex Pain. Alex is highly motivated and committed, always looking for new challenges in life. She enjoys Art in all its forms and she likes British sense of humour – just don’t tell her French fellow countrymen! She is passionate about food and more recently horse riding.

Creative Solutions Director: Elizabeth Cook is an Art lover, Scientist who loves to connect people, semi-pro athlete, and unreasonably entertained by challenges and puzzles. For e-Luminate, she hopes to provide leadership, hard work and out of the box thinking.

Artistic Advisor: Chris Williams. In 2007, after a career as Creative Director in Hertfordshire, Chris moved to Cambridge to start his own independent art gallery. He is also a director of Love Cambridge and takes a keen interest in all things Cambridge, especially development of the arts.

The website luminaries

The web technology developed for this website is open-source, and available from GitHub. The team is available for consultancy.

Team leader: Kim Spence-Jones is a serial entrepreneur, project manager and team leader. He is also Programme Manager for OpenDCU.org, working on open standards for the technology of sustainable smart and connected homes

Django Programmer: Michele Mattioni is responsible for the map technology, and for technical leadership in the project. His day job is Managing Director of the Sustainable Souk

Developer: Jo Paulger implemented a lot of the web site backend and dynamic web pages, and contributed to styling the pages. Jo is currently a volunteer at Aptivate

Graphic design: Philip Pepper is responsible for the artistic design of the website. Philip runs the Pencil and Pepper illustration, graphic design and animation agency.

IT Director: Khalid Sattar is a highly experienced IT manager. He worked on website deployment, management and backup technology.

On the shoulders of giants…
The web team gratefuilly acknowledge the fantastic work of those who have gone before: the Django, Bootstrap and Bootswatch teams in particular.
