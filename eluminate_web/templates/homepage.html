The 2013 e-Luminate Cambridge Festival

20th — 24th Feb

Welcome

The inaugural e-Luminate Festival 2013 was an exciting visual display which showcased one of Cambridge's most upwardly mobile sectors: Clean Technology. In a series of collaborations, Clean Tenchnology innovators and visual artists used light and installation art to demonstrate some of the extraordinary breakthroughs in this modern and environmentally-focussed industry.

Our programme of events ran from the 20th – 24th of February 2013 and included interactive light displays, workshops, talks and events to delight and educate all ages.
 

Watch out for details of upcoming events from the e-Luminate team.
